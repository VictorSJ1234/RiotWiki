Synopsis

League of Legends: Wild Rift is a 5v5 MOBA game developed by Riot Games and released it on October 27, 2020 specifically for mobile PvP fights. It is a 5v5 battle arena where players can show their strength and tactical moves. This can be played with friends and family, for this game is filled with action, thrill, and new features that other similar games don’t have.


Gameplay

Like LoL, Wild Rift has similar gameplay as its sister game. The main difference between the two is the user interface, since Wild Rift is played on mobile. The two teams of five powerful champions have only one goal and that is to break the Nexus on the enemy team. The Nexus is where the player’s allied minions will spawn, and behind of the Nexus is the fountain wherein they can rapidly regenerate ther health and mana, and also to access the Shop. To reach the opposing Nexus, the team must clear at least one lane. Turrets and inhibitors are defense structures that defend the base of each opposing teams. Each lane features three turrets and one inhibitor, with two turrets guarding each Nexus. Turrets deliver damage to opposing minions and champions while also providing their team with limited visibility due to the Fog of War. To dodge damage and rush ahead, those structures, alongside the minions, should be attacked to continue the progress. A Turret surrounds each Inhibitor to keep it safe. Super minions will spawn in that lane for several minutes after being destroyed. The Inhibitor will revive after that, and Super Minions will stop spawning. The jungle, which lies between the lanes, is home to neutral beasts and jungle vegetation. The Drakes and Baron Nashor are the two most powerful beasts. Killing these units provides your team with distinct benefits and can even swing the game around.

Sample gameplay of League of Legends

The optimum squad composition for the game consists of five lanes. Each lane caters to specific champions and roles. Top lane champions are the team's toughest individual fighters. It is their responsibility to defend their lane while concentrating on the opponent team's strongest members. The role of the junglers is to hunt. They sneak between lanes with stealth and skill, keeping a watchful eye on the most crucial neutral monsters and waiting for an opponent to drop their guard. Mid laners are high-burst damage champions who can do it all, both solo and with a team. Combat may not be their specialty, but they will find ways to outdo their enemy. Bot lane champions are the team's dynamite. They must be protected early on as valuable cargo before gathering enough gold and experience to propel the team to victory. Support champions are team guardians. They help keep teammates alive and primarily focus on setting up kills, protecting their teammate in bot lane until they become stronger.

Sample gameplay of League of Legends

As the game proceeds, Champions gain experience and wealth to level up and purchase more powerful things. Overpowering the opponent team and destroying their base requires staying on top of these two aspects. Champions level up as they gain experience, unlocking or strengthening skills and increasing their base stats. Kill enemy units and champions, assist in a kill, and destroy defense buildings to get experience. The in-game money used to purchase equipment for your champion is gold. Kill enemy units and champions, aid in a kill, destroy defense structures, and equip gold income equipment to earn gold. The Shop is where you may use your gold to buy and sell stuff. It's only available if you're at the Fountain. Champions can have up to seven items and five core skills at once. Choosing the right ability order, summoner spells, and item composition for your champion will aid your team's success.

Characters

The player can choose for over 40 champions that is fit to his desired role. The game has six different categories for champions:

  • Assassin - Move rapidly while dealing massive damage to a single target. Quick fighting and jumping to safety are the finest uses for these heroes.
  • Fighter - These champions have a lot of damage dealing and taking abilities. Fighters excel in long-duration battles.
  • Mages - These champions excel in long-distance battles. Mages may do a lot of damage while remaining invulnerable to attacks if they are sufficiently protected.
  • Marksmen - Like mages, Marksmen are best used while doing damage at a distance.
  • Support - The primary function of support characters is to magnify the strengths and abilities of their teammates.
  • Tank – Just like fighters, Tanks are best deployed as a "damage sponge."

Due to the numerous playable champions in the game, one character per category is presented as an example on how these roles are played.

Master Yi (Assassin)

Master Yi has tempered his body and honed his mind to the point where thought and action are practically interchangeable. Though he only uses violence as a last resort, the grace and speed of his blade assures that any conflict is resolved quickly. As one of the last living practitioners of the Ionian art of Wuju, Yi has dedicated his life to upholding his people's legacy, analyzing potential new followers using the Seven Lenses of Insight to select the most deserving.

Skills

Double Strike (Passive)

Master Yi strikes twice after every few basic attacks.

Alpha Strike

Master Yi teleports across the battlefield at breakneck speed, causing physical damage to several units along the way while remaining untargetable. Alpha Hit can critically strike creatures and deal additional physical damage. Alpha Strike's cooldown is reduced by basic attacks.

Meditate

Master Yi rejuvenates his body by concentrating his mind, restoring health and reducing damage for a brief period of time. For each second, he channels, Master Yi will acquire stacks of Double Strike and halt the remaining duration on Wuju Style and Highlander.

Wuju Style

On basic attacks, adds extra true damage.

Highlander

Master Yi moves with incredible agility, improving his Move Speed and Attack Speed while also making him immune to any slowing effects. Highlander's duration is extended if Champion kills or assists while active. On a kill or assist, he reduces the cooldown for his other abilities passively.

Nasus (Fighter)

Nasus is a powerful, jackal-headed Ascended creature from ancient Shurima who is revered as a deity by the desert people. He was a fiercely clever keeper of knowledge and a master strategist whose wisdom lead Shurima's ancient empire to grandeur for millennia. He withdrew into self-imposed exile after the fall of the empire, becoming little more than a legend. He has returned now that the old city of Shurima has risen again, determined to guarantee it never falls again.

Skills

Soul Eater

Nasus absorbs his opponent's spiritual force, increasing his Life Steal.

Siphoning Strike

If Nasus slays his opponent, he deals damage and increases the power of his future Siphoning Strikes.

Wither

Nasus slows down an enemy champion's Move Speed and Attack Speed over time.

Spirit Fire

Nasus summons a spirit flame that deals damage and lowers the Armor of opponents who stand in its path.

Fury of the Sands

Nasus conjures a spirit flame that inflicts damage and reduces the Armor of those who come into contact with it.

Ahri (Mage)

Ahri is a vastaya who can mold magic into orbs of raw energy and is inextricably linked to Runeterra's hidden power. She enjoys playing with her prey's emotions before swallowing their life force. Despite her predatory nature, Ahri retains empathy as each soul she takes provides her with glimpses of memories.

Skills

Essence Theft (Passive)

When Ahri uses her abilities to attack 9 foes, her next ability heals her for each enemy hit.

Orb of Deception

Ahri launches her orb out and then pulls it back, causing magic damage out and true damage back.

Fox-Fire

Ahri receives a short burst of Move Speed and launches three fox-fires at adjacent opponents. Charm (E)

Charm

When Ahri blows a kiss at an enemy, it hurts and charms them, disabling their mobility abilities and allowing them to wander harmlessly towards her.

Spirit Rush

Ahri sprints forward and fires essence bolts at adjacent opponents, dealing damage. Spirit Rush can be cast up to three times before going on cooldown, and while taking down enemy champions, it earns additional recasts.

Jinx (Marksman)

Jinx is a maniacal and rash criminal from Zaun who lives to cause mayhem with little regard for the consequences. She unleashes the loudest blasts and brightest explosions from her armory of lethal weapons, leaving a trail of mayhem and panic in her wake. Jinx despises boredom and delights in bringing her own brand of chaos wherever she goes.

Skills

Get Excited! (Passive)

Jinx's Move Speed and Attack Speed are greatly improved anytime she assists in the killing or destruction of an enemy champion's epic jungle monster or building.

Switcheroo!

Jinx alters her basic attacks by switching between her minigun Pow-Pow and her rocket launcher Fishbones. Pow-Pow attacks offer Attack Speed, but Fishbones attacks deal area of effect damage, have a longer range, but drain Mana and attack slower.

Zap!

Jinx fires a blast with Zapper, her shock pistol, that damages the first enemy struck, slowing and revealing it.

Flame Chompers!

Jinx launches a barrage of snare grenades that explode after 5 seconds, setting foes ablaze. Enemy champions who walk over Flame Chompers will be bitten and rooted in place.

Super Mega Death Rocket!

Jinx launches a super rocket across the map, dealing damage along the way. When the rocket collides with an opponent champion, it explodes, causing damage to it and nearby enemies based on their missing health.

Braum (Support)

Braum is a champion in tank support. With his heavy crowd control, he excels at staying back and defending the team. Unbreakable can block all incoming abilities and auto-attacks, while Stand Behind Me provides extra resistances to an ally and yourself. Braum is extremely effective at all stages of the game due to his defensive abilities and safe kit, which let him to survive and contribute to the squad.

Skills

Concussive Blows (Passive)

Concussive Blows are Braum's primary assaults. Ally basic assaults stack Concussive Blows once the first stack is applied. The victim is stunned and absorbs magic damage after 4 stacks. They cannot gain additional stacks for the next few seconds, but they will absorb bonus magic damage from Braum's assaults.

Winter’s Bite

Braum propels freezing ice from his shield, slowing and dealing magic damage. Applies a stack of Concussive Blows.

Stand Behind Me

Braum leaps to a target allied champion or minion. On arrival, Braum and the ally gain Armor and Magic Resist for a few seconds.

Unbreakable

Braum raises his shield in a direction for several seconds, intercepting all projectiles causing them to hit him and be destroyed. He negates the damage of the first attack completely and reduces the damage of all subsequent attacks from this direction.

Glacial Fissure

Braum slams the ground, knocking up enemies nearby and in a line in front of him. A fissure is left along the line that slows enemies.

Alistar (Tank)

He is a front-line tank for your team and excels at engaging with his considerable crowd control abilities. Before you play Alistar, you should be aware of his crucial combo. Headbutt, Pulverize is the name of this combo. This combines his 2nd and 1st abilities, allowing you to effortlessly knock out an opponent! Going into practice mode on Wild Rift is the greatest method to perfect this combo.

Skills

Triumphant Roar (Passive)

Alistar charges his roar by stunning or displacing enemy champions or when nearby enemies die. When fully charged he heals himself all nearby allied champions.

Pulverize

Alistar smashes the ground, dealing damage to nearby enemies and tossing them into the air.

Headbutt

Alistar rams a target with his head, dealing damage and knocking the target back. May also target turrets to deal bonus damage.

Trample

Alistar tramples nearby enemy units, ignoring unit collision and gaining stacks if he damages an enemy champion. At full stacks Alistar's next basic attack against an enemy champion deals additional magic damage and stuns them.

Unbreakable Will

Alistar lets out a wild roar, removing all crowd control effects on himself, and reducing incoming physical and magical damage for the duration.
